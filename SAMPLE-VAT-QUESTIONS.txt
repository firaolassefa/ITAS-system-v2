Question 1: What does VAT stand for?
Type: Practice
A) Value Added Tax
B) Variable Annual Tax
C) Verified Asset Tax
D) Very Advanced Tax
Correct Answer: A
Explanation: VAT stands for Value Added Tax, an indirect tax on consumption of goods and services

Question 2: VAT is collected at every stage of production and distribution
Type: Practice
A) True
B) False
Correct Answer: A
Explanation: VAT is collected at each stage of the supply chain, from manufacturer to retailer to consumer
Points: 5

Question 3: What is the VAT registration threshold in Ethiopia?
Type: Practice
A) 500,000 Birr

Question 4: Are exports subject to VAT in Ethiopia?
